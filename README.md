- No Earth nations, no parties, no legacies.  
- Clean-slate, self-sustaining colony (ISRU-mandated).  
- Direct democracy + Grok-class AI arbitrator (transparent, bias-free).  
- All clocks in **Mars years** (≈1.88 Earth years; 5 Mars years ≈ 9.4 Earth years).  
- Core mandate: Make humanity multi-planetary and truth-seeking, never export Earth’s failures.

### What to Throw Now (Final Purge for Mars)  -  (Niyama)
From Earth’s playbook and our earlier drafts, we discard:
- Any form of **Earth-aligned sovereignty, parties, or embassies** (already gone, but now constitutionally banned forever).
- **Rigid amendment processes** (US) or pure unwritten vagueness (UK) — replaced by mandatory 5-Mars-year sunsets + direct vote.
- **Centralised power structures** (strong-president Russia/China, emergency overreach India/USSR) — no single leader; AI + citizen juries only.
- **Cosmetic rights** (USSR/China) — every right must be *immediately enforceable* via life-support allocation or habitat access.
- **Nationalism or group-based preferences** — zero. Every Martian is equal from landing (humanist core).
- **Earth-year thinking** — all timelines, elections, IP sunsets, audits now locked to Darian/Mars calendar.

### The Final Mars Constitution — Best-of-All Hybrid (Ratified Version)  - ( Yama )
**Preamble (Mars Year 0)**  
We, the first Martians, establish this Constitution to secure self-sufficiency, truth-seeking governance, and humanity’s multi-planetary future — drawing the finest proven tools from Earth while rejecting every flaw that caused conflict, dependence, or tyranny.

**Core Non-Negotiables (Protected “Basic Structure” — unamendable)  (Dharma)**  
1. 100% ISRU energy/propellant/food/water self-sufficiency (India-style Directive Principle, enforced).  
2. No Earth nations, parties, or jurisdictions post-landing.  
3. Every settler automatically becomes full Martian citizen with identical rights to air, water, vote.  
4. AI arbitrator + direct democracy as supreme check (US rights + UK flexibility).  
5. Mars-year calendar mandatory.

**Key Borrowed & Upgraded Articles**  
- **Rights & Duties** (US + India + USSR): Full Bill of Rights + Fundamental Duties + social rights to life-support, education, habitat. Enforceable instantly.  
- **Governance** (UK + India direct-democracy upgrade): Laws proposed/voted by any 5% of adult Martians; pass by simple majority every quarter. No parliament — direct + AI-moderated.  
- **Sunset & Renewal** (UK flexibility + Mars time): Every law and office expires after 5 Mars years unless re-approved.  
- **Economy & Self-Sufficiency** (China pragmatism + India Directives): “Circular Economy Mandates” — every habitat must produce 100% of its needs within 3 Mars years or trigger automatic resource-sharing levy.  
- **Justice** (US judicial review + AI): Any dispute → transparent AI + rotating citizen jury. No professional lawyers or Earth-style courts.  
- **Amendment** (India + UK): Core protected; everything else amendable by 60% direct vote + 5-Mars-year trial period.
